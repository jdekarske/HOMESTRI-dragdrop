Instructions

In order to ensure consistent shape recognition in the placement of stowage in this station, you must monitor this autonomous robot's behavior. To initiate sorting, consider the pieces of stowage in Intake and the required configuration shown in Instruction. Move stowage from Intake to Robot Command by clicking the stowage piece and the desired position. The grey spaces correspond to positions on the top and bottom of the rack. Throughout the process please adjust your trust in the system using the purple slider beneath the camera feed where the left indicates less trust and the right indicates more trust. Additionally, please report and issues using the Report Robot Issue dropdown menu.
