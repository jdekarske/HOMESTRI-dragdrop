Common Mistakes

You should treat this study like you're sitting in a research laboratory, and give it your full attention! Here are some tips to make sure everything goes as smoothly as possible.

  1. Turn off or silence your phone - you can time out of this experiment if your attention lapses and your screen becomes inactive. You will be ineligible to continue/recieve full compensation if this happens.
  2. Don't hit the 'back' button or refresh. This can easily be done on some laptops if you have a 'two-finger' trackpad shortcut set up. Consider temporarily disabling this shortcut.
  3. If you use multiple workspaces, accidently flipping between workspaces will also log as an inactive screen. This can easily be done if you have a 'three-' or 'four-finger' trackpad shortcut set up. Consider temporarily disabling this shortcut.
  4. Please avoid exiting from fullscreen mode during the experiment. The experiment itself will move you in and out of fullscreen at the correct time.
  5. If you need to use the restroom, now is a good time!

NOTE: Please focus your attention on the computer screen and avoid looking down at your hand throughout the experiment.

Please understand that we reserve the right to not compensate entries that fail to follow instructions.
